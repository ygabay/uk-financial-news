What is the Alternative Investment Market?

The alternative investment market is a broad term that describes all non-traditional investment opportunities. It includes things like hedge funds, private equity, real estate investment trusts and other private equity funds. The alternative investment market is also sometimes referred to as the secondary market or the private equity secondary market. There are many advantages to investing in these funds as opposed to primary markets like stocks and bonds.
