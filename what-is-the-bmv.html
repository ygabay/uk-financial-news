What is the BMV?

The Bureau of Motor Vehicles (BMV) is a government office that governs the operation of motor vehicles. The BMV is also referred to as the Bureau of Motor Vehicles or Bureau of Motor Carriers. The BMV is responsible for registering motor vehicles and issuing driver’s licenses in each state.
